Sashley

Sashley (born June 3, 2006 [age 18]), formerly known as Kushley, is an animator and artist known for her animation memes. She is one of the most well-known users in the animation meme community.

She originally started animating in 2016, her first video being "LONE DIGGER || MEME" , uploaded on March 4, 2017.

Sashley currently uses Adobe Animate CC for her animations, formerly used to animate with Macromedia Flash 8. She uses Clip Studio Paint to draw artwork and drew with a mouse up until 2019-2020, where she started using a Wacom tablet. She now currently uses the Gaomon tablets she's reviewed.

Her main OC is an anthropomorphic ragdoll cat named Ashely.

Ashely (or Ash) is seen wearing a pink sweater and collar, her left eye is blacked out with a purple X pupil, and her hair is half purple and blue.

Mortis has a similar appearance as Sashley, with minor differences. Mortis has white fur, with pink and black hair and black legs. She has black streak marks running down their face, and can sometimes have a censor bar over her face, as seen in the AMV Affection.
